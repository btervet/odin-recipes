Warm Squash Soup

Description

This recipe will make a wonderful warm soup using squash and other late season vegetables perfect for cold winter days. The vegetables can easily be replaced based on local availability.

This dish is endlessly flexible, and can be kepe vegetarian or have shredded chicken added. Toppings can be served separately to allow each individual to make the soup their own.

Ingredients

Soup
  • Butternut Squash - 1
  • Red Top Turnips - 2
  • Bell Peppers (all colors) - 3
  • Sweet Onion - 1
  • Hot Peppers to taste (jalepeno, cerano, etc) - 1
  • Garlic Cloves - 3
  • Vegetable Stock - 2 liters
  • Tomatoes - 4 large
  • Cumin - 4 tbsps
  • Chili Powder - 2 tbsps
  • Salt - 1 tbsp
  • Pepper - 2 tsps
Toppings
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocado
  • Tortilla Strips or Chips

Steps

  1. Peel and Roughly chop squash, turnip, onions, and garlic and add to instant pot.
  2. Chop the rest of the vegetables and add to instant pot, along with spices and stock.
  3. Cook in instant pot on High for 30 minutes.
  4. Release pressure on instant pot.
  5. Use immersion blender to fully blend soup. It should be slightly thick. Add stock if needed to thin soup.
  6. Adjust spices to taste.
  7. Serve with toppings.
